Fitness App Design Brief
Theme: Elite Performance After Dark

The app should feel like a high-end fitness brand—not a loud gaming app and not a sterile medical app. The emotional goal is to make users feel powerful, focused, and progressing every time they open it.

Think:

Nike Training Club meets Batman's precision
Luxury performance car dashboard
Modern athletic wear brand
Confidence, momentum, discipline

Overall Brand Feeling

If users had to describe the app in three words:

Powerful. Disciplined. Premium.

Not "fun."

Not "extreme."

Not "luxury."

The feeling should be:

"This app makes me feel like the type of person who never skips workouts."
