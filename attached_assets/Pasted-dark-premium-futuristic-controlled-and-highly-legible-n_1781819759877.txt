7) Where to use accent color for maximum effect

The biggest mistake would be using cyan everywhere.

It should be used like a spotlight.

Use the accent color on:

checkmarks/icons
important stats
select underlines
hover states

Do not use the accent color on:

every icon on the page

You want the eye to know what matters immediately.

8) Copy emphasis system: bold, underline, highlight

This is where the page will either feel sharp or messy.

Use bold for:
outcome phrases
contrast phrases
mechanism phrases
emotional turning points

Examples:

personalized training and nutrition system
adapts when life changes
not another passive tracker
built for the reasons people usually quit
Use underline for:
only highly strategic phrases
usually 1 per major section
mechanism claims or emotional re-frames

Best use cases:

“It protects momentum.”
“You do not need more information.”
“The goal is making progress easier to sustain.”

Underline style:

not default browser underline
use a custom soft cyan underline or animated brush line
2px to 3px thickness
slightly offset below text
Use accent highlight color for:
short phrases inside headlines
numbers
one pivotal benefit in a paragraph
product name mentions
callout proof

Examples:

build your ideal body faster
adapts in real time
helps you stay consistent without needing to be perfect
Best emphasis rule

In any block of copy:

bold 1–2 ideas
accent-highlight 1 idea
underline 0–1 idea

Never do all three on the same phrase.
